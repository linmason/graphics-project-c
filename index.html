Shading and Lighting Demo

Made by Mason Lin using WebGL

Demonstrates different shading and lighting techniques with customizable light and materials.

Instructions

WASD moves camera around. arrrow keys rotate camera. "M" key changes sphere material

Top row of buttons changes shading and lighting methods. Sliders below change light position, ambient, diffuse, and specular Illumination as well as emissive term, specular exponent, ambient, diffuse, and specular reflectance of the material.
